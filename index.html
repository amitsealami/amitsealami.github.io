I am a PhD Candidate at the Computer Science Department, College of William and Mary, Co-advised by Dr. Denys Poshyvanyk and Dr. Adwait Nadkarni. I am currently researching on Software and Security as part of the SEMERU and SPL research groups.

Using software engineering techniques, I research to create systematic frameworks that can help fix/find security related issues in existing software or approaches.
